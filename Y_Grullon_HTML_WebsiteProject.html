What is Fútbol

Fútbol, known as soccer in some countries, is the world’s most popular and widely played sport. It’s a game of passion, skill, and teamwork, enjoyed by millions of people of all ages across the globe. Played on a rectangular field with two teams of eleven players, the objective is simple—score more goals than the opposing team. But how do they score? The players maneuver a ball with there feet to the oppsing side then kick it inside the goal
